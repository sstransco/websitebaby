Corporate Manifesto

Our Core Mandate

**Sigma Squared Transport Corporation (SSTC)** was founded in 2025 on the principle of maximizing efficiency and financial strength for the long haul. We are more than just a trucking company; we are a logistics technology firm with wheels.

  • > **100% American Capital:** Proudly owned and operated within the United States.
  • > **100% American Labor:** Dedicated exclusively to American employees and drivers.
  • > **Cutting-Edge Technology:** Obsessed with integrating the latest logistics software and AI to achieve unmatched OTR efficiency.
  • > **Bitcoin Treasury Standard:** We are committed to maintaining a Bitcoin-focused corporate treasury for generational value preservation and financial independence. A portion of all profits is automatically used to purchase and hold spot Bitcoin.

SSTC is defining the future of American trucking by combining old-school work ethic with next-gen strategies.
